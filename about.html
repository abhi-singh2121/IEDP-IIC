About Us

IEDUP-IIC – Driving Innovation, Incubation & Entrepreneurship

Our Vision

To position Uttar Pradesh as a hub of innovation and entrepreneurship by fostering startups, MSMEs, and innovators with the best resources, infrastructure, and support ecosystem.

Our Mission

To empower entrepreneurs and innovators by providing incubation, mentorship, financial/legal assistance, and global market access — thereby driving economic growth and job creation.

Objectives

  • Provide holistic incubation support for startups and MSMEs.
  • Create a strong ecosystem of mentors, investors, and industry experts.
  • Enable access to global markets and government schemes.
  • Develop advanced labs and co-working facilities.
  • Promote entrepreneurship and innovation across Uttar Pradesh.

How We Make It Happen

Launchpad

Supporting startups with incubation, prototype development, and market strategies.

Global Market Access

Connecting entrepreneurs with domestic and international markets through B2B/B2C channels.

AI & Tech Labs

Providing state-of-the-art AI-driven labs, IoT facilities, and testing environments.
